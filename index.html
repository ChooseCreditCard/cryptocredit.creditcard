What is crypto-backed credit?

Crypto-backed credit lets you lock up digital assets as collateral while you:

  • • Spend on a Visa/Mastercard credit card
  • • Draw a credit line without selling your coins
  • • Keep potential upside while accessing liquidity

How issuers structure risk

Collateral & LTV

Borrow limits are set as a percentage of your crypto value (loan-to-value), often 30–60% for volatile assets.

Liquidation triggers

If markets move against you, collateral can be partially or fully liquidated to keep the position safe.

Stablecoin buffers

Some programs use stablecoins as an intermediate step to reduce volatility risk on the issuer side.

Regulation & KYC

Full KYC/AML still applies – crypto credit does not remove identity, it just changes where collateral lives.

Rewards model

Rewards may be paid in tokens, stablecoins or traditional cashback depending on the issuer.

When does crypto credit make sense?

Crypto-backed credit can be interesting if you:

  • • Believe strongly in the long-term value of your assets
  • • Need short- to medium-term liquidity without triggering a taxable event
  • • Understand and accept liquidation and volatility risk
